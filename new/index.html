Genetic Disorder

Genetic disorders occur when a mutation affects your genes. Carrying the mutation doesn’t always mean you’ll end up with a disease. There are many types, including single-gene, multifactorial and chromosomal disorders.

About Us

The Orange Support Hub is an innovative platform designed to raise awareness about genetic disorders. We provide accessible, accurate information about genetic conditions, including their causes, symptoms, and treatments, while integrating tools like hospital locators and interactive educational resources.
